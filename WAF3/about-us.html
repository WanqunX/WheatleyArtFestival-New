About us

The annual Wheatley Arts Festival is a one-of-a-kind festival and takes place in the picturesque village of Wheatley. Bathing in the light of Oxford, the arts festival takes a great deal of inspiration from the local city. Any art enthusiast visiting oxford will know how much of a hub this region is for art with other famous galleries such as Modern Art Oxford and the Ashmolean. In addition to the amazing galleries close by, we also have great links with Oxford Brookes University.

Wheatley Arts festival will be offering prestigious works of art from around the world, with a bit of something for every enthusiast. We will be hosting dancing, live music, sculptures, conceptual art, ceramics and even an auction where one lucky attendee will be able to win a free watch. Alongside our fantastic event offerings, we will be accompanied by three leading experts: Lucy Hare, Hank Richards and Christina Sato - they are keen and will host meet-and-greets with fans.

Best of all, we can offer all of this - for absolutely nothing! We are able to run this festival free of charge with all profits from the auction going to a local artist’s grassroots charity. So, what’s the catch one may ask? Well, due to our exclusivity we can only offer 1000 tickets which are on a first-come-first-serve basis. So why wait? Join us at the most exclusive arts festival in Oxford and book your free ticket today!
