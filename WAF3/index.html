About us

The annual Wheatley Arts Festival is a one-of-a-kind festival and takes place in the picturesque village of Wheatley. Bathing in the light of Oxford, the arts festival takes a great deal of inspiration from the local city. Any art enthusiast visiting oxford will know how much of a hub this region is for art with other famous galleries such as Modern Art Oxford and the Ashmolean. In addition to the amazing galleries close by, we also have great links with Oxford Brookes University.
